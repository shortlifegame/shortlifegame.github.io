Gameplay Short Life

Short Life game is a huge playground where every step may become your last one. Watch where you are going and pay extra attention to all the signs you see during your risky journey. If the game tells you to wait before going somewhere, you better listen if you don’t want to be brutally killed!

Meet Hilarious Characters

Initially, your hero in Short Life is a simple homeless man, but the further you traverse through Short Life, the more amusing personages you can obtain. Get acquainted with all the townies with their dark humour and exceptional appearances and try not to kill them while leading them through a series of perilous hurdles.

However, to acquire new heroes in Short Life you will need to gather a certain number of stars. Pick them up while passing the levels and spend cautiously, as you don’t get a lot of them! In some cases, you may even need to explore hidden areas of the game, so be attentive and check out any suspicious passages, items or mechanisms.
